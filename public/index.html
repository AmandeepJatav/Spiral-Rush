About Spiral Rush
The world's first real-time multiplayer spiral maze racer

What is Spiral Rush?

Spiral Rush is a competitive online maze game where 2–3 players race through the same circular spiral maze in real time. Navigate from the outer edge to the colorful center — the fastest player wins.

Real-time multiplayer

Every match is live. No bots, no AI — only real players from around the world. Watch your opponents' progress in real time as you race to the center.

Play with friends

Create a private room, share the 6-letter code, and race your friends. Private rooms support up to 4 players. Or jump into global matchmaking for a random challenge.

Compete globally

Climb the global leaderboards, earn pro ranks from Bronze to World Champion, unlock achievements, complete daily missions, and prove you're the fastest maze solver on the planet.

How to Play
Simple to learn, impossible to master

Getting started

1

Create your profile — pick a username and country when you first join.

2

Choose match size — select 2-player or 3-player mode.

3

Wait for players — the match starts when all slots are filled.

4

Race to the center — navigate the maze and reach the colorful balls first!

Controls

DESKTOP
Arrow keys / WASD
MOBILE
Virtual joystick

Move in any direction — up, down, left, right. Your ball follows the maze paths toward the center. The controls move your ball relative to the screen, not the maze rotation.

Winning & ranking

  • Fastest solve time wins — reach the center before your opponents
  • Rankings update live — any player can overtake anyone
  • Global Score combines speed, win rate, consistency, and level
  • Pro ranks from Bronze → World Champion based on performance
  • Daily maze — same maze worldwide, compete for the fastest daily time

Features to explore

  • Private rooms — invite friends with a room code (up to 4 players)
  • Ghost runs — race against your best time or the world record
  • Cosmetics — unlock avatars, borders, and titles through skill
  • Achievements — 12 badges to earn across different challenges
  • Seasons — 3-month competitive seasons with fresh rankings
